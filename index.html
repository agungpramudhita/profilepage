Research focus

Four strands
Learning

AI in education & learning analytics

Learning management systems, serious games, and augmented-reality tools designed so that student behaviour leaves a readable trace — step counts, timing, and log data used to understand how learners think, not just what they score.

IoT

Internet of Things & smart systems

Sensor-driven systems for healthcare queues, fire and crash detection, smart grids, and water access — built for low-resource settings where reliability matters more than novelty.

Data

Applied machine learning & decision systems

Forecasting, classification, and multi-criteria decision support applied to agriculture, health screening, digital forensics, and business siting problems.

Vocational

Vocational & technology-enhanced learning

Curriculum design, life-based and work-based learning models, and teacher capacity building for polytechnic and vocational education in Indonesia.
